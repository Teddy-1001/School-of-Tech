School Fee Structure

School of Technology provides a world-class learning experience and the best quality education for your child at an affordable price. We’re always available if you have any questions or wish to discuss any aspect of the finances.

We understand that choosing the right school for your child is a significant decision, and we want to ensure that you have all the information you need to make an informed choice. Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and competitive, reflecting the high-quality education and facilities we provide.

Fees Structure

Our tuition fees are structured to provide flexibility and value for our students. We offer various payment plans to accommodate different financial situations. For detailed information on our tuition fees, please contact our admissions office or visit our website.

Additional Information

  1. Annual fees for years 11 and 13 will be paid in two terms of equal installments in September and January.
  2. Parents who wish to pay on termly basis need to contact the school in advance.
  3. Transport within 10kms radius from the school is KSHS 30,000. One way transport is 50% of KSHS 55,000.
  4. School fees is paid in full before the start of every term and is not refundable. The second option is payment in two installments (50% before school opens and 50% on or before the 15th of the following month).
  5. Payment in USD will be based on the prevailing market exchange rate of Kenya Shilling to the dollar.
  6. A parent who may wish to withdraw a child from the school will need to give one term’s notice, otherwise one term fee is charged.
  7. Family discounts on tuition fees only:
    • 2nd Child – 10%
    • 3rd Child – 20%
    • 4th Child – 30%
    • 5th and subsequent Child – 40%
  8. Transport discount: 50% for second and subsequent child if on the same route.
  9. Fees are paid through bank accounts. No cash is accepted. Use Paybill or bank transfer and send proof to accounts email or WhatsApp. Always indicate student name and year.
  10. The Aviation Program is paid separately upon registration into the program.
  11. A surcharge of 2% per month is levied for late payment of fees.
  12. Clinical fee covers nursing services, insurance against accidents, and hospital logistics.
  13. External exams are paid separately in Sterling pounds. Students must clear fees before sitting exams.
